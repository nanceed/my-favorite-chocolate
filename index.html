Why Dandelion Chocolate?

Two Ingredients. That's It.

Since our earliest days, we've focused on bringing out the nuances within really good cocoa, using only two ingredients: ethically sourced, well-fermented, carefully roasted beans and organic cane sugar. To allow the most flavors to shine through, we don't add cocoa butter, soy lecithin, vanilla, flavorings, or preservatives—all commonly used in chocolate making.

We have found that good relationships are absolutely essential to procuring consistently good flavor. That’s why, year after year, we strive to strengthen our relationships with cocoa producers by traveling to each origin. We work closely with the producers who grow, ferment, and dry the single-origin cocoa beans we source, so we can be confident we’re sharing some of the best and most distinctive cocoa with you.

San Francisco Locations

16th Street Factory

Address:

2600 16th Street
San Francisco, CA 94103

Details:

After five years of planning and renovations, we opened our 16th Street Chocolate Factory in 2019. The century-old brick building, converted from a former print shop, covers almost a city block near the north edge of San Francisco’s Mission District. Inside, visitors inhale the heady scent of molten chocolate, hear cocoa beans rattle and roast, watch chocolate makers in action, sift handfuls of dried beans, and sample every form of cocoa product — from tangy fresh cacao fruit to smooth finished chocolate. Our 16th Street Factory is the new heart of Dandelion chocolate making, where single-origin cocoa beans become two-ingredient chocolate.

Valencia Street

Address:

740 Valencia Street
San Francisco, CA 94110

Details:

We opened our first factory and café on Valencia Street in 2012 to share the bean-to-bar chocolate-making experience with visitors. We bought, built, and repurposed machinery to perform every step of the process, and constructed an open space where guests may enjoy a front-row seat. To this day, we invite Valencia Street visitors to come in and stay a while — sample our single-origin chocolate bars, savor a steaming mug of House Hot Chocolate, and watch our chocolate makers at work.
With working factory equipment on view, our café at Valencia makes a terrific setting for casual celebrations. Guests are served fresh pastries and café beverages; additional refreshments may also be requested.

Ferry Building

Address:

1 Ferry Building
San Francisco, CA 94111

Details:

San Francisco’s historic Ferry Building is an architectural landmark that has housed a gourmet food hall since 2003. In 2014, we joined the Ferry Building's artisan makers, opening a Marketplace shop to showcase local collaborators’ creations and offer our own bean-to-bar chocolate. Stop by for gifts, chocolate bars, hot chocolate drinks, and freshly toasted s’mores.
Our Kitchen team bakes fresh cookies and pastries from our single-origin chocolate each day. We also offer made-to-order hot chocolates, Ritual coffee drinks, frozen hot chocolate, horchata, and more.

Fillmore Street

Address:

2115 Fillmore Street
San Francisco, CA 94115

Details:

We've always wanted a location on Fillmore Street. In 2022, we were able to sign a short-term lease for a holiday retail pop-up at 2115 Fillmore Street. Sanga Architects’ Monica, who has played a leading role in designing many of our previous shops, returned with Anh to design and build this space for our guests. Fillmore will soon undergo renovations to become a permanent location. Late this summer, we will close for a few months, and expect to be up and running by the holiday season.
Stop by our holiday pop-up on Fillmore and sample single-origin chocolate or find a special gift from our retail display.
